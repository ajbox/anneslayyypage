THE GIST


view comments from all over the web and try to guess what the post was about.

it's helpful to start vague in your guesses like guessing a noun:

  • "person"
  • "place"
  • "thing"

after guessing a noun try to guess an overrall topic such as music, food, sports, etc.

guessing the platform and type of post may be helpful as well.


finally, answers typically are more than one word and sometimes combine close guessed words

AND REMEMBER, these comments are sometimes related but generally random to the actual post, so get creative while guessing ;)
