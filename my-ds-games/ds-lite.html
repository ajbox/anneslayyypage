The Nintendo DS Lite is "Stylish gaming on the go with a colour to match your every mood. The lighter and brighter version of the original Nintendo DS. Use the stylus directly on the Touch Screen. Backwards compatible with all Game Boy Advance games"

The Nintendo DS Lite is a foldable handheld game console developed and manufactured by Nintendo. It is the second iteration of the Nintendo DS and is slimmer, brighter, and more lightweight than the original. It was announced on January 26, 2006, more than a month before its initial release in Japan on March 2, 2006, due to overwhelming demand for the original model.
